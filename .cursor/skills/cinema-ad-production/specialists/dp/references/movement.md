# Camera movement

| ID | Movement | Duration fit | Notes |
|----|----------|--------------|-------|
| `move:static-observe` | Locked | Any | Joe default |
| `move:push-in-slow` | Slow dolly in | 2s+ end of shot | Reveal pause beat |
| `move:pull-back-slow` | Slow dolly out | Scene end | Release after hold |
| `move:pan-follow-hands` | Gentle pan | 3–5s | Follow pour, reach |
| `move:handheld-subtle` | Micro handheld | Ernesto friction | Not shaky-cam |
| `move:crane-down` | Modest descent | Avoid Joe | Too performative |

## Rules

- One move per shot max for clips under 5s
- Push only on story beat (pause, realization)
- Never crane/orbit witness object as hero reveal
