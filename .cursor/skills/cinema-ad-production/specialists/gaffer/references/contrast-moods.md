# Contrast moods

| ID | Ratio | Mood | Use |
|----|-------|------|-----|
| `contrast:low-warm` | Low | Comfort, morning | Joe domestic |
| `contrast:medium-natural` | Medium | Neutral reality | Ernesto friction |
| `contrast:high-noir` | High | Tension | Rare; brief must ask |
| `contrast:flat-overcast` | Very low | Memory, quiet grief | Time passage |

## Time passage lighting

Same location different time:

| Story beat | Shift |
|------------|-------|
| Morning → afternoon | Warmer, harder angle |
| Day → evening | Practical lamp on, window blue |
| Years passage | Same window, softer wear, lower sun angle |

Document in continuity_locks via gaffer notes.
